QUALITY TIME

When it comes to Gary Chapman's five love languages, quality time is the one that centers around togetherness.
It's all about expressing your love and affection with your undivided attention.

When you're with your partner, you put down the cell phone, turn off the tablet, and focus on them.
And, when you do that, it touches their heart in a way that really matters.
They feel important, loved, and special—like you were intentional in setting aside time just for them.

How to Give Your Partner Quality Time

1) Set limits on technology.
2) Plan something different.
3) Focus on quality, not quantity.
4) Use active listening skills.
5) Make eye contact.
6) Develop a routine.
