RECEIVING GIFTS

Of all the love languages, gift-giving and receiving is arguably the most
often misconstrued. To some, it can seem greedy, or as if the recipient is
fixated on things versus love itself. But that’s not the case.
Williams explains that, in this sense, the true meaning of gift giving isn’t
extravagance—it’s sentiment. A person with this love language might
cherish the gift, however small, more than someone who speaks a
different love language. Every time they see it, it serves as a reminder that
they are loved.

What to Know About Your Partner's "Gifts" Love Language

1) Even if gifts is not your love language, you need to learn it if it's your partner's!
2) Present act as little reminders that you are always thinking of them
3) They don't need to be big purchases or all the time
4) Be sure to give a gift on big occasions
